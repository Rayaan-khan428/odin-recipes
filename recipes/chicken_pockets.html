Chicken Pockets

Description

This foil packet recipe is versatile, and you can modify it based on your preferences and the
you bring. You can also add other vegetables like carrots or zucchini, or swap out the chicken for
fish, beef, or tofu, depending on dietary preferences.

Ingridients

  • 4 Breast Chicken Pieces
  • 10g Sweet Tomatoes
  • 8oz beans
  • Mince 4 cloves
  • 1 Lemon sliced

Steps

  1. Season Chicken with salt, pepper and whatever other seasonings you like
  2. Throw in the rest of the ingridients next to the chicken
  3. Wrap it all in tinfoil and crimp it shut
  4. Throw on the grill or on your campfire for 30 minutes
